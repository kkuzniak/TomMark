The Frontline Club is an agency with an extremely rich history of supporting independent and reliable journalism. It helps organise access for journalists to the most dangerous regions of the world, such as the Gaza Strip and Syria. It also provides an effective platform from which to support diversity and professionalism in the media, promotes safe practices, and encourages both freedom of the press and freedom of expression worldwide.
